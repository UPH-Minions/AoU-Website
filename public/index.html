Ambassadors of UPH


Ambassadors of UPH is a program with the purpose of developing people who have the determination and heart to serve God and others. The Ambassadors are figures equipped with compassion and willingness to bring impacts from within to the society. This year, Ambassadors of UPH 2021 is coming back by raising the tagline, “IRIDESCENT” to express that no two people are exactly alike. Yet, we are all created in God’s image and all the potentials that lie within us shall be embraced in hope that they will inspire and impact the lives of others.
