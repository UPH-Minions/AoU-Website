Our Projects

Ambassadors of UPH Medan 2019's Project/Campaigns

Rethink Your Drink

A campaign presented by the Ambassadors of UPH Medan 2019 to raise the awareness of millennials to lessen their intake of unhealthy drinks, which may contain preservatives.

Share The Joy

A campaign raised by the Ambassadors of UPH Medan 2019 to answer the challenges of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) which emphasizes more on No Poverty and Zero Hunger by making sure that people out there, especially children and the more vulnerable, have access to sufficient and nutritious food.

Blossom From Home

During the challenging times of COVID-19 outbreak, the Ambassadors of UPH Medan 2019 executed a campaign called Blossom From Home in hope to inspire the surroundings to be empowered during the quarantine. Through the campaign, the Ambassadors shared about their tips on self care, self development and productive activities.

Road to 10K

Road to 10K is an event where students are given the chance to start a healthier lifestyle even during unprecedented times in pandemic. Just as the name suggests, the Ambassadors of UPH Medan 2020 want to gather students from UPH Medan Campus to virtually walk together, and reach 10K steps together along with the Top 12 Ambassadors of UPH Medan 2020.

Mercharity

A project that focuses on sharing the joy of giving. Through the sales of every merchandise of Mercharity, every profit earned was donated to Panti Asuhan Pintu Harapan.

Discover Inside You (DIY) *ongoing

A webinar and workshop organized by the Ambassadors of UPH 2020 in collaboration with the Public Speaking Club of UPH Medan where students will be given new knowledge and experience to know themselves more and to improve their own potentials to boost their confidence.

We Hear You (WHY) *ongoing

We Hear You (WHY) is a program by the Ambassadors of UPH Medan 2020 in which students and relevant guest speakers of UPH Medan Campus will be invited to discuss the most up-to-date and happening issues at the moment. The Ambassadors of UPH Medan 2020 will take actionable measures or movement in response to the current issues being discussed. Each month, there will be one episode aired that will cover relevant and interesting issues.
