We Hear You (WHY)

We Hear You (WHY) is a program by the Ambassadors of UPH Medan 2020 in which students and relevant guest speakers of UPH Medan Campus will be invited to discuss the most up-to-date and happening issues at the moment. The Ambassadors of UPH Medan 2020 will take actionable measures or movement in response to the current issues being discussed. Each month, there will be one episode aired that will cover relevant and interesting issues.
